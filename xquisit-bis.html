Bahamas Information Services Website
Web Design

Bahamas Information Services (BIS) is a politically-neutral department that links Bahamians with their government and provide a primary channel for correspondence. They needed a website that could serve as a central hub of information, while offering people the opportunity to connect with the right individuals within the government and voice their concerns and opinions.

The design visually conveys connectivity and resourcefulness, with a clear national affiliation. Subject-oriented imagery was used to create visual interest and help relieve users of the density of information.
