Business Women of International Trade 2019 Newsletter
Print

Each year, Business Women of Internation Trade (BWIT) publishes an annual newsletter to communicate expanded trade opportunities, advice and valuable information to Canadian business women involved in international trade.

The 2019 design capitalized on colour and geometric shapes to maintain consistency with other BWIT collateral. Imagery, background textures, and corporate fonts were used strategically to promote the exchange of knowledge, intelligence and resources.
