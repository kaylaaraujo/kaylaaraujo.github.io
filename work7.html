YouAreHere for the Rideau Centre
UX/UI

YouAreHere is an app designed for the newly renovated Rideau Centre, a shopping centre in the heart of downtown Ottawa. It was created to solve the problem of not being able to find a mall map in this large and expanding mall. The user can select stores to create the most direct route to save time and saved for future visits. Facilities and centre services are also included on the map.
