Business Women of International Trade 2018 Newsletter
Print

Business Women of International Trade (BWIT) is a program run through Global Affairs Canada. It provides targeted support and services to Canadian women entrepreneurs and helps encourage their international growth by assessing market potential, finding qualified contacts, and resolving business problems.

While keeping with their 2018 branding guidelines, this newsletter was carefully formatted and designed to be clean and legible, yet dynamic and visually interesting in both English and French.
