Hi!

I'm a Designer!

I’m Kayla — Graphic Design and Advertising graduate from Algonquin College. I have lived in Ottawa, Canada my whole life and I have been itching to seek new and beautiful adventures in the world of design.

Why Design?

Being able to solve a problem visually and coming up with new ways to communicate emotions and ideas is exciting. And there are so many ways to create! Design is limitless.

I love simplistic design that is functional, has a clear conceptual idea, and draws the attention of people using its enduring personality.

At the end of the day, I truly believe that life’s problems can always be solved in a beautiful and creative way.
