Innovative Medicines Canada: 2017 Annual Report
Print

Innovative Medicines Canada, guided by a Code of Ethical Practices in the pharmaceutical industry, works with governments, insurance companies, healthcare professionals and stakeholders to advance the field and enhance the wellbeing of all Canadians.

Once the inital concept was created and approved by the client, it was my responsibility to flow and format all written content and carry the look and feel throughout the production of both English and French print and interactive documents. Imagery was carefully chosen to appropriately reflect the content and additional graphic elements were strategically placed to create a playful uniformity.
