Canadian Nuclear Association: 2018 Conference Identity
Branding

The Canadian Nuclear Association (CNA) holds a yearly conference for companies and organizations across the nation to gather and learn about current events, new technologies, and opportunitiesin the nuclear industry. In 2018, the theme of the conference was The New Generation

The main focus of this logo is to show progress and a forward-thinking attitude. The arrows are representative of the shift in the perception of the nuclear industry, in which nuclear is seen in a more positive light.
