About Me

I’m Kayla and I’m an Ottawa-based designer — a designer who thinks conceptually but isn’t afraid to experiment with execution. I’m a designer who loves typography and believes that every ascender, serif, and terminal has an emotional power over us. I’m a designer that embraces new tricks to improve my trade. I’m a designer whose late work nights include eating sugary cereal to stay awake because I don’t really like coffee. I’m a designer who will only buy a book if it has a nicely designed cover because it’s more than just a piece of literature, it’s a piece of art. And that’s what I love about what I do. I’m a designer, it’s true. But I’m more than that. I’m an artist.
