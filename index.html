About Me

Hi, I’m Kayla and I’m a sushi-loving craft show enthusiast and graphic designer.

I love purposeful design – clean, effective and eye-catching. I work with my clients to understand their wants and needs, exploring all options and developing the best possible design solution.

When I’m not designing, I love discovering new small businesses to support and finding creative ways to reduce my ecological footprint.

I am an advocate for shopping sustainably and helping the economy. That’s why it’s a major goal of mine to work with more small business owners to help them grow and connect with their community.

I’m open to any size project you got. Whether it’s a full web design or a simple stationery set, I’m excited to work with you. Let’s get started!

What I Do

Print

Print work leaves the customer with something they can touch and explore. Attractive print work is kept around providing the opportunity to engage an audience for a longer period of time.

Branding

Brand design is one of the most important parts of a successful business strategy. Having a strong identity in today's competitive markets will send any business soaring through the sky.

Front End Design

We live in a digital age where most people find their information through the Internet. Having a well-designed site is an integral part of attracting an audience and having a successful business.

UI/UX

The most successful apps are simple and direct in both design and function. Keeping information uncluttered, understandable and as user-friendly as possible is what I always strive to accomplish.
