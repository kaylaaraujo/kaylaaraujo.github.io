Innovative Medicines Canada: 2018 Annual Report
Print

Innovative Medicines Canada is a membership of 44 companies that revolutionize Canadian healthcare through research and discovery and the development of new medicines and vaccines. They believe that Canadians must have access to necessary innovative treatments and that their efforts are important for safeguarding the Canadian healthcare system for future generations.

Similar to the 2017 Annual Report, my role in this project was to apply the established concept to both English and French print and interactive documents. The brand typefaces have been used to provide continuity throughout the report and with all other IMC collateral. Full-page application of imagery offered interesting contrast to the cover and provided the opportunity for pause within a content-heavy document.
