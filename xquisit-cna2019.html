Canadian Nuclear Association: 2019 Conference Collateral
Branding

The 2019 conference theme was New Nuclear: Generating Solutions for Climate and Health.Attendees found out how it generates solutions for the big challenges of today and tomorrow and why the young generation is becoming passionate about new nuclear.

For this project, I was responsible for determining the look and feel and designing all conference materials based on the corporate identity created by another Xquisit Communications team member. Conference materials included: large format pop-up banners, conference room signage, Westin Hotel key cards, programs, name tags, video displays, and mobile app graphics.
