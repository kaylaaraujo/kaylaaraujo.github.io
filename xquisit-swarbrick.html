Swarbrick Law Rebrand
Branding

Swarbrick Law is a collaborative law firm offering family law services since 1997.In 2017, Swarbrick Law decided to modernize their visual identity.

In this joint-effort and multi-faceted solution, a modern, simple approach embraces several different concepts – two L’s taken from Law are arranged to symbolize unity while two outward-facing arrows represent diverging parties protecting an inner core. Further, the shape can evoke the theme of light at the end of the tunnel.

Business stationery, outdoor signage, and a website refresh were also created to implement the new corporate identity.
