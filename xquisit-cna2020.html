Canadian Nuclear Association: 2020 Conference Identity
Branding

The 2020 theme for the Canadian Nuclear Association's annual conference was Nuclear Now: Achieving our Clean Energy Future. The conference's primary purpose was to promote nuclear energy as an eco-friendly and reliable energy source, a clear path for the future of power generation.

To communicate the conference's theme and bring attention to the global fight to tackle climate change, I designed an identity that consisted of foliage shapes encircling a power button to represent the positive impact renewable energy has for the planet. The CNA blue, light green and dark green colours were implemented as a gradient to strengthen the visual connection to the CNA brand and enhance the modernity of the identity.
